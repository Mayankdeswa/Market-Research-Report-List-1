<p><p>Micro-LED display technology is an advanced display solution that utilizes microscopic LED elements to create images. Unlike traditional LEDs, Micro-LEDs are self-emissive, meaning each pixel generates its own light, resulting in enhanced color accuracy, high brightness, and improved energy efficiency.
