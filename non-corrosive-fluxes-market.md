<p><p>The non-corrosive fluxes market is primarily divided into two types: resin flux and organic flux. Resin flux, typically made from natural or synthetic resins, provides excellent adhesion and is widely used in soldering electronic components, ensuring minimal residue and corrosion risk. Organic flux, derived from plant or animal sources, offers effective soldering capabilities while being environmentally friendly. Both types are essential for maintaining the integrity of electronic devices, as they enhance the soldering process without causing damage to sensitive components.</p></p>
