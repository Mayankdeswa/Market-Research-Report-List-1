<p><strong>The Visible Light Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave</li><li>Pulse</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The visible light fiber laser market is primarily segmented into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulse lasers. Continuous Wave lasers emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring steady output, such as cutting and engraving. In contrast, Pulse lasers operate in short bursts of energy, generating high peak power for precision tasks like marking and micro-machining. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, with CW lasers suited for continuous operations and Pulse lasers excelling in detailed, high-precision applications.</p></p>
