<p><p>The fish pond impurity filter market comprises three primary types: physical, chemical, and biological filtration. Physical filtration involves the removal of solid particles, debris, and sediment using mesh screens or filters. Chemical filtration employs substances such as activated carbon to absorb harmful chemicals and impurities from the water. Biological filtration utilizes beneficial bacteria to break down harmful waste products, thereby maintaining water quality and supporting aquatic life. Together, these filtration methods ensure a healthy environment for fish and aquatic plants.</p></p>
