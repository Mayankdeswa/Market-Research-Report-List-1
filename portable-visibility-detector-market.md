<p><strong>The Portable Visibility Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perspective</li><li>Scattering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable Visibility Detector market can be categorized based on two main types: perspective and scattering. Perspective detectors assess visibility by analyzing clarity and distance based on atmospheric conditions, making them suitable for applications like aviation and transportation. Scattering detectors, on the other hand, measure the degree of light scattering caused by particles in the air, providing more precise visibility data. Together, these technologies enable improved safety and navigation in low-visibility environments such as fog, smoke, or pollution.</p></p>
