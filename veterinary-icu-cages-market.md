<p><p>Veterinary ICU cages are essential for the intensive care of animals, and they come primarily in two market types: monoblock and parallel. Monoblock type cages are constructed as a single unit, providing enhanced stability and easier cleaning, making them ideal for critical care environments. In contrast, parallel type cages consist of multiple, interconnected units that allow for more flexible space utilization and easier access to multiple patients simultaneously. Both designs focus on optimizing animal welfare and facilitating veterinary care.</p></p>
