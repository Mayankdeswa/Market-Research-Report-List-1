<p><strong>The Automotive Hub Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inner-rotor Motor</li><li>External-rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hub motor market features two main types: inner-rotor and external-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors have the rotor located inside the stator, providing compact designs and efficient heat dissipation, making them suitable for high-performance applications. In contrast, external-rotor motors feature the rotor on the outside, allowing for larger diameters and enhanced torque at lower speeds, ideal for applications needing high torque output. Both types contribute to the growing trend of electric vehicles by enabling efficient, direct wheel propulsion.</p></p>
