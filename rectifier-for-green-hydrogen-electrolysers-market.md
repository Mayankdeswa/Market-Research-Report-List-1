<p><p>In the green hydrogen electrolysers market, rectifiers are crucial for converting AC power to DC required for electrolysis. SCR (Silicon Controlled Rectifier) rectifiers offer high efficiency and reliability, suitable for large-scale electrolysis applications. They are known for their robustness and ability to handle high power levels. On the other hand, IGBT (Insulated Gate Bipolar Transistor) rectifiers provide greater control over power modulation and higher switching frequencies, making them ideal for dynamic and flexible electrolysis processes. Together, they play vital roles in optimizing hydrogen production.</p></p>
