<p><p>The dyes and pigments market comprises various types, each serving specific applications. Disperse dyes are primarily used for synthetic fibers, offering a wide range of vibrant colors. Reactive dyes bond chemically with fabrics, making them ideal for cotton and other cellulosic materials. Sulfur dyes are favored for their high fastness and cost-effectiveness in dyeing textiles. Vat dyes provide excellent lightfastness, suitable for cotton and wool. Acid dyes are mainly used for protein fibers and nylon, providing rich colors suitable for different textiles.</p></p>
