<p><ul><li>Multimode</li><li>Single-mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi-Wavelength Laser Module market is divided into two main types: multimode and single-mode. Multimode lasers support multiple light paths within the fiber, enabling high data throughput over shorter distances, making them suitable for local area networks and short-distance communications. In contrast, single-mode lasers allow only one light path, promoting higher efficiency and greater distances, ideal for long-haul telecommunications. Each type caters to specific applications, influencing market demand based on required performance and distance capabilities.</p></p>
