<p><p>High Precision Optical Time Domain Reflectometers (OTDRs) are advanced testing instruments primarily used in fiber optic networks to analyze signal integrity and identify faults within the fiber. These devices utilize the principle of Rayleigh scattering to provide detailed insights into the optical fibers' performance, enabling precise measurements of cable length and the location of imperfections. </p><p>The market for High Precision OTDRs is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for high-speed broadband connectivity and the expansion of telecom infrastructure globally.
