<p><p>The HgCdTe infrared detector market includes three primary cooling types: </p><p>1. **LN2 Cooled**: Utilizes liquid nitrogen for cooling, offering high sensitivity and performance in low-temperature applications, popular in research and military sectors. </p><p>2. **Thermoelectrically (TE) Cooled**: Employs thermoelectric devices to achieve moderate cooling, balancing performance and power efficiency for portable applications. </p><p>3. **Stirling Cooled**: Uses a Stirling cryocooler for efficient cooling, combining long operational life with high reliability, suitable for demanding environments like defense and space.</p></p>
