<p><strong>The Automotive Keyless Go Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Keyless Go market is divided into two main types: Passive Keyless Access (PKES) and Remote Keyless Access (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physical contact by detecting the key fob within proximity, enhancing convenience and security. RKES, on the other hand, requires the user to press buttons on the key fob to lock, unlock, or start the vehicle from a distance. Both systems are increasingly adopted for their ease of use and advanced security features.</p></p>
