<p><p>The Spine Orthosis System market encompasses two primary types: Inlay Type and Externally Assisted orthoses. Inlay Type systems are designed for direct integration within the patient's clothing or support structures, offering discreet spinal support without bulk. In contrast, Externally Assisted orthoses are external devices that provide visible support and stabilization, often featuring adjustable components for a custom fit. Both types aim to enhance spinal alignment, alleviate discomfort, and promote recovery in patients with various spinal conditions.</p></p>
