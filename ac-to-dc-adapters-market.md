<p><p>The AC to DC adapters market comprises mainly two types based on the connector configuration: Center Positive (+) and Center Negative (-). Center Positive adapters have the positive voltage on the center pin, commonly used for laptops and some electronics, ensuring correct polarity for optimal device performance. Conversely, Center Negative adapters feature the negative on the center pin, typical for certain audio equipment and older devices. Understanding these types is crucial for selecting the right adapter to prevent damage and ensure compatibility.</p></p>
