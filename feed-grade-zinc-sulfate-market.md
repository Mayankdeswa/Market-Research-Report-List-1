<p><p>The feed grade zinc sulfate market primarily consists of two types: zinc sulfate monohydrate and zinc sulfate heptahydrate. Zinc sulfate monohydrate, containing one water molecule, is favored for its higher purity and solubility, making it ideal for livestock nutrition. In contrast, zinc sulfate heptahydrate, with seven water molecules, is often used in bulk applications due to its cost-effectiveness and stability. Both forms serve as essential zinc supplements in animal feed, supporting growth and health in livestock and poultry.</p></p>
