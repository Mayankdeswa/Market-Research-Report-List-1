Brass instruments, like trumpets and trombones, generate sound by the player's lip vibrations in a brass mouthpiece. Percussion instruments, including drums and tambourines, create sound through striking or shaking. Each type has unique characteristics and appeals to different musical styles and preferences, contributing to a diverse musical landscape.</p></p>
