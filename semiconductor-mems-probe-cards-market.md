<p><strong>The Semiconductor MEMS Probe Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical MEMS Probe Cards</li><li>Cantilever MEMS Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor MEMS probe cards market is segmented primarily into vertical and cantilever MEMS probe cards. Vertical MEMS probe cards utilize a vertical configuration, allowing for high-density probing and better alignment, making them suitable for advanced packaging and miniaturized devices. In contrast, cantilever MEMS probe cards leverage a spring-like structure that provides flexibility and adaptability for varying chip designs, enhancing performance in multi-site testing. Both types are essential for efficiently assessing semiconductor functionality and improving manufacturing processes.</p></p>
