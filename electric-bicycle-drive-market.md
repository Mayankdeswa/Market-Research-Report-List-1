<p><ul><li>Hub Motor</li><li>Center Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric bicycle drive market features two primary types: hub motors and center motors. Hub motors are integrated into the wheel hub, offering simplicity and low maintenance, making them popular for urban commuting and leisure riding. In contrast, center motors are positioned near the bike's crank, providing better weight distribution and enhanced performance on varied terrains. This design allows for efficient power transfer and improved climbing capabilities, appealing to more serious cyclists and those seeking advanced riding experiences.</p></p>
