<p><strong>The New Energy Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The New Energy Vehicle Wheel Speed Sensor market features primarily two types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type sensors. Hall Type sensors utilize a magnetic field to identify wheel rotation by generating voltage signals, providing precise speed measurement. In contrast, Magnetic Electric Type sensors rely on the variation of magnetic fields induced by the wheel's movement, enabling speed detection through induced voltages. Both types are crucial for enhancing vehicle safety, stability control, and overall performance in electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
