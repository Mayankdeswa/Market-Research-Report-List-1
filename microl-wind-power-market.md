<p><p>The micro wind power market is primarily categorized into two types: horizontal axis wind turbines (HAWT) and vertical axis wind turbines (VAWT). HAWTs are characterized by their propeller-like design and are typically more efficient in capturing wind energy, making them suitable for higher wind speed areas. In contrast, VAWTs have a more compact design, allowing for operation in turbulent wind conditions and easier maintenance. Both types cater to small-scale energy generation, promoting renewable energy adoption at the local level.</p></p>
