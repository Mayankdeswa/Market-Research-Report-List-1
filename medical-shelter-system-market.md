<p><p>The Medical Shelter System market is categorized into two primary types: Soft Medical Shelter Systems and Hard Medical Shelter Systems. Soft Medical Shelters are typically composed of lightweight, flexible materials, making them quick to deploy and highly portable, suitable for temporary field hospitals or emergency medical response. In contrast, Hard Medical Shelter Systems are constructed from durable, sturdy materials, providing long-term stability and protection, often used in permanent medical facilities or challenging environments where enhanced security and resilience are essential.</p></p>
