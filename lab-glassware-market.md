<p><p>The lab glassware market is categorized into four primary types: containers, measurers, filters, and others. Containers include various vessels such as beakers, flasks, and test tubes designed to hold and store liquids. Measurers consist of graduated cylinders and pipettes used for precise liquid measurements. Filters encompass funnels and filtration apparatus for separating solids from liquids. The 'others' category includes specialty glassware like petri dishes and culture plates, catering to specific analytical or experimental needs in laboratory environments.</p></p>
